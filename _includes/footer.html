About this Site

This fan site is dedicated to Borussia Dortmund’s first womens squad. Our ladies are playing their first season in the seventh tier of German football, in the Kreisliga A (Group 2). I’ll be providing squad information, statistics, and match reports (as much as I possibly can) as the team begins its climb up the German football pyramid.

This site is not affiliated with Borussia Dortmund.
