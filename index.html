Additional Coverage

  • BVB Frauen Photos by BVB-Spielplan.de: Great photos from our friends at BVB-Spielplan.de (@TinneffBlog on Twitter).
  • BVB Frauen Podcast Translation by Julia Poblotzki: On August 5, 2021 Borussia Dortmund published a podcast episode with Svenja Schlenker (Borussia Dortmund’s Department Head, Womens Football), Lisa Klemann (Borussia Dortmund Frauen player), and Thomas Sulewski (Borussia Dortmund Frauen head coach). The podcast, of course, is in German. But Fear the Wall’s Julia Poblotzki (@jpoblo on Twitter) graciously translated it to English for the site. Thank you, Julia!
  • Get to know our BVB Women’s Team: Annika Herbig: She was one of the three scorers in BVB's first women’s match on August 8. What a fantastic start that was: In front of a crowd of 1,300 fans in the Stadium Rote Erde, the Black & Yellows triumphed 3-1 (2-0) over the also newly-formed women's team of TSV 1860 Munich.
  • S1 Ep.25 Bleav in Borussia: Exclusive English Interview with Svenja Schlenker, Head of BVB's new women's team + Fan Chat Kansas City: Tilo was able to secure and interview in English with Svenja Schlenker, BVB’s Head of Women’s Football. In this episode we learn:
    • Svenja has always been banging the drum for a women’s team, but things really started to heat up in 2019 as the club had no good answer for why they didn’t have a team yet.
    • About 80% of club members surveyed wanted to start from the bottom up rather than acquiring a license.
    • About 150 players tried out for the team.
    • All 24 players are Borussia Dortmund fans.
    • And more…
  • Borussia Dortmund launches "Girls' and Women's Football" department: Original announcement of the establishment of a women’s football department at Borussia Dortmund. (September 10, 2020)
